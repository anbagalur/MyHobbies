My Hobbies

Drawing

Unlike the other two, drawing is a pretty recent interest of mine. I am still not very good with a pencil or brush but I am trying to learn. Sometimes I just enjoy making small doodles and patterns and basic scenery. Shading is also something I like to do. 

Reading books

I have loved reading from a pretty young age. I just can't wait to have new books to read. It is my favorite to pass time. I have read books by many authors but few I love to read the books of. Sudha Murthy is my all time favorite authors, the Harry Potter series is one of my favorite series and i also liked reading Black Beauty by Anna Sewell   

Chess

Though I am a beginner, I really enjoy playing chess. I often play with my father but sometimes also with my mother. Chess is a challenging but fun game. It is important to plan out every move. It is my favorite indoor game
